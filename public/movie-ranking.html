The Best Movies according to Bruce

A few of my favorite movies

Second Hand Lions

This is a movie about a young boy being left with his uncles while his mom goes to look for work. The young boy gets to hear about his uncles adventures, and he ends up writing about them.

The Sandlot

Local boys start on a baseball adventure when they invite the new kid to their neighborhood baseball game, and end up have the biggest game of pickle any of them have seen.

The Blind Side

A movie about a family taking in a troubled youth and making him part of the family. Based on a true story. The young man becomes an NFL player.
